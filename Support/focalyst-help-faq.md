# Help & FAQ

## Getting Started

**What is Focalyst?**
Focalyst is an all-in-one productivity app that helps you manage tasks, build habits, focus deeply with Pomodoro timers, capture notes, and track your progress — all in one place. It is powered by Gemini AI to give you personalised productivity insights.

**How do I create an account?**
Open Focalyst and tap "Create account" on the welcome screen. You can sign up using your Google account or with your email address. First-time users are guided through a short onboarding flow to personalise the experience.

**Is Focalyst free to use?**
Yes. Focalyst has a free plan that includes unlimited tasks, 2 habit trackers, 4 Pomodoro presets, 1 countdown, 5 notes, basic weekly insights, and 5 AI chat messages per day. Upgrade to Pro or Lifetime for unlimited access to all features.

**Which devices can I use Focalyst on?**
Focalyst is a web application that works on any browser on mobile and desktop. It is optimised for mobile screens. A native Android and iOS app is coming soon.

## Plans & Billing

**What plans does Focalyst offer?**

**How do I upgrade to Pro?**
Go to your Profile page by tapping the avatar icon in the top-left corner. Tap "Upgrade Plan" inside the My Plan card. Select your preferred plan and complete payment via Razorpay. Your plan activates instantly after payment.

**What payment methods are accepted?**
Payments are processed securely by Razorpay. You can pay using UPI, debit card, credit card, net banking, or wallets.

**Can I cancel my Pro subscription?**
Yes. Tap your avatar → My Plan → Manage Plan → Cancel Subscription. Your Pro access continues until the end of your current billing period. We do not offer refunds for the unused portion of a subscription.

**What happens to my data if I cancel?**
All your tasks, habits, notes, and history remain saved in your account. You simply lose access to Pro features and return to free plan limits. Your data is never deleted when you downgrade.

**Does the Lifetime plan include future features?**
Yes. Lifetime plan holders get all future features included at no extra cost, including the upcoming mobile app and App Blocker feature.

**I was charged but my plan did not activate. What do I do?**
Contact us at admin@chintan.shop with your payment receipt. We will resolve this within 24 hours.

## Tasks & Habits

**How do I add a task?**
On the Plan tab, tap "+ Add new task" or tap the + button in the centre of the bottom navigation and select "Task". Enter the task title, set an optional due date, priority level, and reminder, then tap Save.

**How do I mark a task as complete?**
Tap the checkbox to the left of the task name. Completed tasks are removed from your list and counted in your Insights.

**Can I reorder my tasks?**
Yes. Press and hold a task row and drag it up or down to reprioritise your list.

**What is the difference between a task and a habit?**
A task is a one-time to-do item. A habit is a recurring activity you want to do every day or on specific days of the week. Habits track your streak — how many days in a row you have completed them.

**How does the streak work?**
Your streak increases by 1 each day you complete a habit. If you miss a day, the streak resets to 0. Streaks are shown on the Insights tab.

**Why can I only add 2 habits on the free plan?**
The free plan includes 2 active habits. Upgrade to Pro for unlimited habits.

## Focus (Pomodoro Timer)

**What is a Pomodoro timer?**
The Pomodoro technique is a time management method where you focus for a set period, then take a short break, and repeat. Focalyst supports custom durations and multiple sets.

**What are the preset options?**
- 25 minutes focus / 5 minutes break × 4 sets
- 45 minutes focus / 10 minutes break × 3 sets
- 60 minutes focus / 15 minutes break × 2 sets
- 90 minutes focus / 30 minutes break × 2 sets

Free plan users can only use these presets. Pro users can set any custom duration.

**Can I use custom focus durations on the free plan?**
No. Custom durations require a Pro or Lifetime plan. The input fields are locked on the free plan with a "Upgrade to Pro" indicator.

**Will my screen stay on during a focus session?**
Yes. Focalyst uses the WakeLock API to keep your screen active during an active Pomodoro session so the timer is never interrupted.

**What is a Day Countdown?**
A countdown is a card that shows how many days remain until an event — for example, an exam, deadline, or trip. Free plan supports 1 countdown up to 30 days away. Pro supports unlimited countdowns at any future date.

## Notes

**How do I create a note?**
Go to the Notes tab and tap "+ New note", or tap the + button in the bottom navigation and select "Note". A blank editor opens immediately.

**Does Focalyst support rich text formatting?**
Yes. The note editor supports bold, italic, underline, headings (H1, H2), bullet lists, numbered lists, and checklists.

**Can I use voice to write notes?**
Yes. Tap the microphone icon in the note editor toolbar to start speech-to-text. Speak naturally and your words appear at the cursor position. This uses your device's built-in speech recognition — no data is sent to any external server. English only.

**Are my notes saved automatically?**
Yes. Notes auto-save every 30 seconds and also save when you navigate away from the editor. You will see a "Saving..." indicator at the top of the screen while this happens.

**Can I download my notes?**
Yes. Open the three-dot menu on any note and tap "Download as PDF". A PDF file is generated on your device and downloaded to your browser's default download folder.

**Why can I only have 5 notes on the free plan?**
The free plan includes 5 notes. Upgrade to Pro for unlimited notes. Your existing notes are never deleted when you reach the limit — you simply cannot create new ones until you upgrade.

## AI Chat

**What can the AI assistant do?**
The Focalyst AI assistant is powered by Google Gemini. It can add tasks for you, set reminders, summarise your week's productivity, answer general productivity questions, and give personalised suggestions based on your data.

**How do I open the AI chat?**
Tap the chat icon in the top-right corner of any screen. On mobile it opens as a bottom sheet. On desktop it opens as a right-side panel.

**Does the AI have access to my personal data?**
The AI receives a summary of your current tasks, habits, and focus data to give you relevant answers. This data is passed securely through our server and is never stored by Google or used to train AI models.

**How many AI messages can I send per day?**
Free plan: 5 messages per day (resets at midnight). Pro and Lifetime plans: unlimited messages.

**Why is the AI giving me generic answers?**
Make sure you have some tasks and habits set up — the AI's responses are more personalised when it has your data to reference. Try prompts like "How productive was I this week?" or "What should I focus on today?"

## Account & Privacy

**How do I edit my name or profile type?**
Go to your Profile page (tap the avatar in the top-left corner) and tap "Edit Profile". You can change your display name and profile type (Student, Freelancer, Creator, Working Professional, Entrepreneur).

**How do I turn off notifications?**
Go to Profile → Preferences. You can toggle Task Reminders, Habit Reminders, and Pomodoro Alerts on or off individually.

**How do I delete my account?**
Go to Profile → scroll to the bottom → tap "Delete my account". You will be asked to confirm. Deleting your account permanently removes all your data including tasks, habits, notes, and insights. This action cannot be undone.

**How do I log out?**
Go to Profile → scroll to the very bottom → tap "Log out".

**I forgot which email I used to sign up. What do I do?**
If you signed up with Google, try signing in with "Continue with Google" — it will match your Google account automatically. If you used email, contact us at admin@chintan.shop.

## Technical

**Focalyst is not loading. What should I do?**
Try these steps in order:
1. Refresh the page
2. Clear your browser cache (Settings → Clear browsing data)
3. Try a different browser (Chrome or Safari recommended)
4. Check your internet connection
5. If the issue persists, contact us at admin@chintan.shop

**My data is not syncing across devices. What should I do?**
Make sure you are logged in with the same account on both devices. All data syncs through Supabase in real time. If data is missing, try logging out and logging back in.

**Speech to text is not working.**
Speech to text uses your browser's built-in Web Speech API. Make sure you have granted microphone permissions to the browser. This feature works best in Google Chrome. It is not supported in all browsers — if your browser does not support it, a message will appear.

**Is Focalyst available offline?**
Currently, Focalyst requires an internet connection. An offline mode with local caching is planned for a future update.
